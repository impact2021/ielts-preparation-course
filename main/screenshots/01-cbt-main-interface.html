Reading Passage 1

The history of technology spans from the first use of stone tools approximately 2.5 million years ago to the present day. Throughout history, humans have discovered and invented many tools and techniques to help them survive in harsh environments and improve their quality of life.

The invention of the wheel around 3500 BC was a crucial development that paved the way for modern transportation. Following this, the development of writing systems allowed for the recording and sharing of knowledge across generations and civilizations.

The printing press, invented by Johannes Gutenberg in the 15th century, revolutionized the dissemination of information. This technology enabled mass production of books and documents, ultimately leading to increased literacy rates throughout Europe.

In more recent times, the Industrial Revolution marked a significant turning point in human history. The development of steam engines and mechanized manufacturing transformed societies from agrarian to industrial-based economies.

Today, digital technology dominates our world. The internet, artificial intelligence, and cloud computing have fundamentally changed how we work, communicate, and access information.
