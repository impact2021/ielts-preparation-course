## üìñ How IELTS Tests Should Be

### ‚úÖ Appropriate IELTS Characteristics:
- Academic vocabulary accessible to educated readers
- Topics of general academic interest
- Technical terms explained in context
- 10-15 specialized terms per passage maximum
- No assumption of subject-specific expertise
- Passages from books, journals, magazines

### ‚ùå What Makes Tests Too Hard (Test 20 Example):
- Requires specialized subject knowledge (chemistry, physics)
- 45+ specialized terms (3x normal density)
- Multiple cross-disciplinary topics simultaneously
- Technical processes not explained adequately
- Assumes background knowledge not in text
