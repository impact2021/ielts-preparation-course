**Old Way:**
1. Click "Check Sync Status"
2. Summary appears (then disappears after 1.5s)
3. Click "Out of Sync" tab to filter

5. Look at individual lesson/resource/exercise status
6. Try to remember which courses have issues

**New Way:**
