## IELTS Reading Test Standards

### What Makes an Appropriate IELTS Academic Reading Test?

**Standard Structure:**
- 3 passages of increasing difficulty
- 40 questions total
- 60 minutes
- Approximately 2,000-2,750 words total

**Content Guidelines:**
- Topics of general academic interest accessible to educated readers
- Vocabulary should be challenging but not overly specialized
- No assumption of prior subject-specific knowledge required
- Passages from books, journals, magazines, and newspapers
- Topics: science, technology, society, culture, environment, education

**Vocabulary Standards:**
- Academic vocabulary (IELTS band 6-9 level)
- Technical terms should be explained in context
- Specialized jargon should be minimal
- No requirement for domain-specific expertise
