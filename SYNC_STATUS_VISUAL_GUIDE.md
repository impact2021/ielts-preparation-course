## Responsive Behavior

### Desktop (Wide Screen)
- Table shows all subsite columns side by side
- Summary cards displayed in a single row (4 columns)
- Full content item titles visible

### Tablet (Medium Screen)
- Table may scroll horizontally for many subsites
- Summary cards may wrap to 2 rows (2 columns each)
- Content item titles may truncate

### Mobile (Small Screen)
- Table definitely scrolls horizontally
- Summary cards stack vertically (1 column)
- Indentation reduced for better space usage
